## Key Initiatives

### 1. Code Modernization Synergy Task Force
We'll assemble a cross-functional tiger team to assess the codebase and develop a comprehensive modernization strategy. Deliverables will include:
- PowerPoint presentation (50+ slides)
- Executive summary (for executives)
- Detailed roadmap (dates TBD)
- Risk register (comprehensive)

### 2. Offshore Development Partnership (Elbonia)
I've identified a cost-effective development partner in Elbonia who can handle the actual coding work. Their rates are incredibly competitive, and they assure me they have "many skilled C programmers."

### 3. Agile Transformation (With Modifications)
We'll implement Agile, but with some common-sense modifications:
- Daily standups (now 90 minutes, with status reports)
- Sprints (8 weeks, for proper planning)
- Story points (converted to hours for tracking)
- Retrospectives (optional, if time permits)
